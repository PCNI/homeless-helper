What is Homeless Helper




The Homeless Helper app provides near real-time information to the people who need it. Information such as shelters near your location, bed availability at those shelters, nearby places to eat a meal and much more.

You can use the full featured web app in a desktop browser or in a mobile browser.


Please spread the word.



Overview




Discover resources nearby or at a different location, Homeless Helper serves all 50 states!




Share with others. Manage your shelter bed availability.



Non-Smartphone Access


Our goal is to provide this information to whoever needs it, anywhere, anytime and on any device.

Homeless Helper currently runs on most smartphone platforms and devices capable of browsing the web.

We also built a voice and SMS service that offers users the ability to discover nearby shelters from any cell phone or touch-tone phone.


Voice access - Call 732-372-HELP (4357) from any touch-tone phone and enter a zipcode when prompted

SMS access - Send a text message to 732-372-HELP (4357) with an address or zipcode


Both of these methods will enable anyone to learn about nearby shelters.



Data


Today, Homeless Helper serves data for the entire United States.

With over 3,000 shelters nationwide, 10,000 medical clinics and thousands of other resources; people in every part of the country are able to find resources nearby.

Every object is geocoded and searchable by location.

Homeless Helper receives data input from several sources: OpenHMIS, Self-registration, Homeless Helper API and other internet resources

The cloud is our infrastructure

Homeless Helper's backend is powered by Digital Ocean and Amazon Web Services.


Mapping and directions capabilities are integrated with the Google Maps API

We utilize Twilio for handling SMS and voice based queries



You can monitor our system health at homelesshelper.status.io
