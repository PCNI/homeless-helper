FAQ




Can people without smartphones access shelter information?

Yes!

Simply send a text message to 732-372-HELP (732-372-4357) with the name of a city. You will receive an automatic response with the nearest shelter's contact information and current bed availability.

There is also an automated voice system. Call 732-372-HELP (732-372-4357) and enter a zip code to locate nearby shelters.



What devices can be used to access Homeless Helper?

To meet our goal of everyone being able to access this information, we have developed solutions for almost every device and platform.



I have an idea, complaint, suggestion. Who should I contact?

Drop us a line at wecare@homelesshelper.us



What is the source of the data?

Data comes in from self-registrations and the OpenHMIS platform.

Learn more about OpenHMIS at www.openciss.org
